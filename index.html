📊 Amazon Product Performance Analysis

Understanding Success Factors in E-commerce

Power BI Status: Complete Year: 2024 Dataset: 1.5K Products

📋 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes product performance across the Amazon India Marketplace, examining 1,462 products across 9 categories to identify success factors in e-commerce. The analysis focuses on customer engagement, ratings, pricing strategies, and discount patterns.

📊 Data Source

Amazon India Marketplace dataset from Kaggle

🎯 Dataset Scope

1,462 products across 9 categories

💡 Business Objective

Identify success factors in e-commerce performance

🛠️ Tool

💡 Key Insights

1.

75.85% of products achieve 4-5 star ratings, indicating a high-quality product catalog overall

2.

Higher-rated products command significantly higher prices (₹3,587 vs ₹518), showing customers are willing to pay more for quality

3.

81% of products use modest discounts (0-20%), suggesting a conservative pricing strategy rather than aggressive promotions

4.

Electronics, Computers & Accessories, and Home & Kitchen dominate the product mix, representing 97% of all products

5.

Musical Instruments shows the highest customer engagement (44,441 ratings per product), 1.5x higher than Electronics
